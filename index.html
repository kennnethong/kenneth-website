About

Executer. Thinking. Builder.

I'm an investment banker based in Singapore. My work spans large-scale capital raises, buy-side advisory, and IPO execution, with particular depth in data centers, AI-driven infrastructure, and platform businesses across Asia-Pacific. Born in the States, grew up in China, now operating out of Singapore.

I think in systems, strategy, and long-arc outcomes. My goal is to transition from advising on transactions to building and operating technology businesses. I'm drawn to the intersection of AI infrastructure, financial systems, and platform-scale commercial logic. Everything I build is a step toward that.

The best builders understand both the strategic layer and the mechanical layer. I don't want to be a developer. I want to be an operator who can build, someone who understands markets, systems, and incentives deeply enough to create things that matter.
